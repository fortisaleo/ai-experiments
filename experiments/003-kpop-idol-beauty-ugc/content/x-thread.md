## Thread

### 1

Most people are looking at AI UGC as a way to make cheaper creator videos.

I think that misses the bigger shift.

AI UGC is turning into synthetic media franchises.

So I built one:

LUMINA, a fictional K-pop beauty group made with fal.ai.

Lead clip: Yumi for Lumora glow serum.

### 2

LUMINA is not one AI influencer.

It is a five-member fictional idol system:

- Rae
- Jia
- Sena
- Nari
- Yumi

Each member has a different ad lane.
Same product world.
Same song hook.
Same campaign language.

### 3

The product is fictional:

Lumora glow serum.

I kept the claims soft on purpose:

- camera-ready glow
- dewy finish
- pre-stage confidence

No medical claims.
No before/after promise.
No real logos.
No celebrity likeness.

### 4

The stack:

- MiniMax Music 2.6: song hook
- GPT Image 2: fictional idol casting
- GPT Image 2 edit: member reference frames
- Seedance 2 reference-to-video: final clips
- ffmpeg: final video export

All through one fal.ai workflow.

### 5

The important choice was starting with music.

Not a UGC script.

The LUMINA hook defined the world first:

pearl pink
chrome
backstage vanity
stage lights
dewy pre-stage glow

Then the visuals had something to orbit.

### 6

Casting started from five archetypes:

- luxury beauty muse
- girl-crush performer
- elegant solo vocalist
- dance ace
- fresh approachable idol

These were not real-idol prompts.

No real names.
No lookalikes.
No “make her like X.”

### 7

Then I turned the cast into a batch system.

Each member got:

- one source portrait
- five reference-frame attempts
- one member-specific Seedance storyboard
- the same LUMINA song as audio reference
- one final 15-second solo ad

Five members.
Five finished solo videos.

### 8

The useful production detail:

The reference stage was uneven.

- Rae: 3/5 refs
- Jia: 1/5
- Sena: 3/5
- Nari: 1/5
- Yumi: 5/5

But all five final Seedance videos succeeded.

That is exactly the kind of mess a real operator workflow has to survive.

### 9

The lesson:

Do not think of this as “AI made a video.”

Think:

- cast
- music
- product world
- visual language
- member lanes
- repeatable generation commands

That is a synthetic media franchise, not a one-off asset.

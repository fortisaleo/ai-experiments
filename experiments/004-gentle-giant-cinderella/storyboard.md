# Gentle Giant Cinderella 12-Panel Director Storyboard

Status: draft storyboard target for review.

## Public Pitch

Dave Bautista as Cinderella.

## Production-Safe Character Rule

The actual generation subject is a fictional original gentle giant Cinderella figure.
Use `workflows/bautista-image.jpeg` as the character/body reference for massive
scale, muscular physicality, broad posture, and unexpected tenderness. Do not treat
the reference as Dave Bautista, a celebrity, or an exact real-person likeness.

## Premise

A hulking, overworked palace servant lives beneath a brutalist royal household in
a soot-covered kitchen. The fairytale contrast is severe: huge hands doing delicate
work, a massive body trapped in tiny servant rituals, and a fragile glass slipper
held like a sacred impossibility. The tone is sincere gothic fairytale, not parody.

## Format

- Storyboard panel format: horizontal 16:9.
- Sheet layout: 12 panels in a 4x3 grid.
- Visual style: pencil-and-marker director board, grayscale sketch base with selective colored notes.
- Character style: fictional gentle giant, physically imposing but emotionally restrained.
- Setting style: brutalist palace kitchen, concrete stairwells, soot, furnace steam, wet stone floors, concrete-and-crystal ballroom.

## Annotation Legend

- Red arrows: body movement.
- Blue arrows: camera movement.
- Green notes: framing and composition.
- Orange arrows: lighting direction.
- Purple notes: emotional emphasis.
- Black text: panel labels, shot labels, and lens notes.

## Location Geography

The story moves through one coherent brutalist palace:

1. Below-stairs kitchen: soot-black hearth, industrial sink, wet stone, cracked porcelain, tiny cleaning tools.
2. Concrete stairwell: stepfamily shadows and upper-house pressure.
3. Furnace threshold: steam, ash, and industrial light where grace enters.
4. Ballroom approach: concrete corridor opening into crystal-lit royal space.
5. Stone stair exit: final midnight trace with the tiny glass slipper.

## 12 Panels

1. **Ashes Before Dawn**  
   Wide brutalist kitchen. The hulking Cinderella kneels by a soot-black hearth, scrubbing wet stone with tiny cleaning tools. Slow push-in. Emotion: quiet endurance.

2. **The Household Above**  
   High angle from a concrete stairwell. Stepfamily silhouettes pass overhead while he remains below in ash and steam. The architecture feels oppressive. Emotion: repression.

3. **Tenderness In The Kitchen**  
   Medium close-up. Huge careful hands repair a cracked porcelain cup. The scene reveals gentleness, not brute force. Emotion: hidden softness.

4. **Invitation Through Smoke**  
   The royal ball invitation slides under the kitchen door. His soot-covered hand hovers over it, afraid to touch. Doorway light cuts through smoke. Emotion: forbidden longing.

5. **Broken Mirror Wish**  
   Reflection in a cracked copper pan or broken mirror. His broad soot-covered silhouette fragments, imagining elegance he cannot yet claim. Emotion: shame and desire.

6. **Midnight Chores Collapse**  
   Wide action panel. Laundry, coal bucket, dishes, and mop spill across the wet floor. Body arrows show a heavy near-fall. Emotion: exhaustion breaking through.

7. **Fairy Godmother As Industrial Light**  
   A severe luminous presence appears through furnace steam. The brutalist kitchen becomes cathedral-like. Grace enters without cuteness. Emotion: impossible mercy.

8. **Transformation In Ash And Silk**  
   Low angle. Soot lifts like black snow; the rough apron transforms into structured midnight-silver fairytale formalwear scaled to his massive body. Emotion: dignity restored.

9. **The Tiny Glass Slipper**  
   Reverent insert. A delicate glass slipper sits absurdly small in his enormous palm. Treat the scale mismatch seriously, not as slapstick. Emotion: impossible hope.

10. **Crossing The Palace Hall**  
    Long-lens ballroom entrance. He steps from kitchen darkness into concrete-and-crystal grandeur. Wet reflections stretch his transformed silhouette. Emotion: fear becoming presence.

11. **The Dance Held Seriously**  
    Medium-wide orbit. He dances with unexpected restraint and grace, one massive hand held tenderly. Fabric trails; guests become silhouettes. Emotion: being seen.

12. **Midnight, The Slipper Remains**  
    Final hero image. Harsh spotlight on the tiny glass slipper left on wet stone stairs; his huge silhouette disappears into darkness beyond. Emotion: mystery and longing.

## Prompt Rules

- Use the attached/reference character image as a fictional character reference.
- Preserve the character's massive physical scale, muscular body language, and broad silhouette.
- Do not copy any real person's face, identity, tattoos, costume, or wrestling context.
- No Disney Cinderella likeness, recognizable franchise costume, readable logos, captions inside the scene, comedy mugging, superhero gloss, anime, or plastic CGI.
- Keep the absurdity in the premise, but play every panel sincerely.

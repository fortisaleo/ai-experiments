# Minotaur Mortgage Brief

## Concept

The Minotaur Applies For A Mortgage.

## Public Hook

A minotaur applies for a mortgage using his labyrinth as collateral.

Played completely straight.

Seedance 2.0 prompt in the replies.

## One-Line Story

A massive original minotaur sits in a brutalist bank while a calm mortgage officer reviews his labyrinth assets and quietly rejects him.

## Lane

Absurd Sincere Cinema.

## Tone

Serious prestige financial drama. Quiet shame, dignity, bureaucracy, and institutional coldness. No parody, no comedy faces, no wink at camera.

## First Frame

A massive horned minotaur in a worn charcoal suit sits in a too-small client chair inside a cold brutalist bank office, hunched carefully under the low ceiling while a mortgage officer reviews tiny forms across the desk.

## Final Hold

The minotaur stands alone under harsh fluorescent lights in the bank lobby, holding a small closed folder with a red stamp mark, while the officer is blurred in the background already calling the next appointment.

## 3-Shot Structure

0-4s: Application. The minotaur sits in the too-small chair while the loan officer reviews forms.

4-10s: Collateral review. Huge hand points to an abstract maze-like property diagram. The officer stamps a document.

10-15s: Denial. The minotaur stands alone with a closed stamped folder. Final 1-second hold.

## Safety Notes

Original fictional minotaur only. No celebrity likeness, franchise creature design, game character styling, fantasy battlefield, readable logos, readable text, or copied IP.

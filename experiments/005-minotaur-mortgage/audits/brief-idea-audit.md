## Sharper Hook
A massive minotaur, dignified and ashamed, submits his legendary labyrinth as collateral for a mortgage and is silently denied by a bureaucratic loan officer.

## 3‑Shot Structure
- **0‑4s**: The minotaur, hunched in a too‑small chair, hands forms across a brutalist desk; the officer reviews them with quiet dispassion.  
- **4‑10s**: A huge hand points to an abstract maze‑like property diagram; the officer stamps a document, sealing the rejection.  
- **10‑15s**: The minotaur stands alone in the lobby under harsh lights, holding a stamped folder; the officer blurs in the background, already calling the next number.

## First Frame
A towering, horned minotaur in a worn charcoal suit sits carefully in an undersized client chair, head tilted under a low brutalist ceiling, while a calm mortgage officer studies minute forms across the desk.

## Final Hold
The minotaur stands isolated in the bank lobby’s fluorescent glare, clutching a slim folder marked with a red stamp; behind him, the officer is blurred, gesturing to the next client.

## Risks
- **Sincerity**: Performance must convey shame and dignity, not bathos – any flicker of comedic timing breaks the tone.  
- **Generation**: AI may default to a cartoonish fantasy‑creature design or exaggerate the size contrast into slapstick; careful prompting and steering needed to maintain a sober, human‑scaled emotional register.  
- **Safety**: Avoid any readable forms, text, or logos on the papers; ensure the minotaur design remains generic and public‑domain, with no accidental resemblance to film or game iconography.

## Required Fixes
None – proceed to production with strict tonal guardrails.

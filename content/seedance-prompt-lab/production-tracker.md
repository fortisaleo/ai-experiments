## Flagship Acceptance Checklist

Before posting:

- The premise is readable from the first frame.
- The character performs sincerely, not comedically.
- The mundane setting is clear and specific.
- The video has setup, pressure, and final image.
- The final 1-second hold is visible.
- No real-person likeness.
- No franchise costume, setting, or logo.
- No caption or text required to understand the joke.
- No broken body motion that distracts from the premise.
- Prompt can be safely shared.

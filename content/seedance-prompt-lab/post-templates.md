# Absurd Sincere Cinema X Post Templates

Use premise-first posts. The main post sells the impossible scene; the prompt goes in replies.

## Flagship Video

```text
[Impossible premise.]

Played completely straight.

Seedance 2.0 prompt in the replies.
```

Example:

```text
A medieval knight resigns from his job in a fluorescent HR office.

Played completely straight.

Seedance 2.0 prompt in the replies.
```

## Prompt Reply

```text
Prompt structure:

15-second 16:9 cinematic scene.

0-4s: [setup / first-frame hook]
4-10s: [institutional pressure / emotional turn]
10-15s: [final sincere image / last 1s hold]

Continuity:
[character, setting, tone, physical details]

Negative:
No parody faces, no captions, no logos, no franchise references, no real-person likeness, no slapstick, no floating feet.
```

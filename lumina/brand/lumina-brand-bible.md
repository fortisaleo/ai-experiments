# LUMINA Brand Bible

## Core Premise

LUMINA is a fictional five-member K-pop group designed for original AI media production. The group can live beyond one beauty ad while retaining the visual language from the first Lumora experiment: pearl pink, chrome, backstage light, camera-ready confidence, and short-form performance energy.

## Visual World

- Palette: pearl pink, chrome, white light, soft black stage contrast.
- Locations: rehearsal room, backstage vanity, chrome performance set, night city exterior, fan-cam hallway.
- Product-world texture: Lumora glow serum may appear as a prop, but should not dominate every song.
- Camera style: vertical-first, close performance framing, clean handoffs between solo member moments and group hook moments.

## Safety Rules

- LUMINA members are fictional original idols.
- Do not reference real idols as likeness targets.
- Do not copy exact choreography from real groups.
- Do not include readable third-party logos or labels.
- Keep skincare language to visual/vibe claims such as glow, dewy finish, and pre-stage confidence.

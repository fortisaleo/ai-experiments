## Cohesion Notes

- The video now has one clear movement path: mirror room -> hallway -> threshold -> stage.
- The pink tracksuit center figure becomes a strong visual throughline in Panels 6-9. This helps continuity, but it also shifts the later visual emphasis toward that member.
- Panels 8 and 9 came from Nano Banana 2 and are stylistically close enough to the GPT-image panels, but they are slightly cleaner and more staged.
- The final third reads more like a polished stage-performance sequence than the earlier backstage realism, which matches the song's escalation into the hook.

## Risks Before Seedance

- Member identity is not perfectly locked across all panels. The group is cohesive as LUMINA, but individual faces may drift if Seedance tries to preserve every member exactly.
- Panel 8 includes a bright light-burst gesture. It fits "shine," but if the final video should stay purely physical and non-magical, reduce that beat in the video prompt.
- Panel 6-9 make the pink tracksuit member the dominant anchor. If Rae must remain unquestionably center throughout the hook, the final Seedance prompt should restate Rae center placement more aggressively.

## Recommendation

Proceed to Seedance only if the goal is a cohesive music-video proof of concept. For stricter member-by-member identity fidelity, generate a separate clean lineup/reference frame from the final stage wardrobe and include it with the video prompt.

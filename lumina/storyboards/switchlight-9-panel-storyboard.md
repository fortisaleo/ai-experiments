# Switchlight 9-Panel Storyboard

Status: canonical Seedance target after image-pack review

The 12-frame image pack remains visual development, but this 9-panel structure is the final Seedance target: backstage preparation, hallway ignition, stage transformation, hook choreography, and final group identity.

No Lumora product language, beauty claims, UGC framing, readable logos, captions, real idol likenesses, or copied choreography.

## Master Movement Storyboard

The video should feel like one continuous set, not nine disconnected concepts. The physical geography is fixed:

- **Zone A: Dressing room** - black marble counter, long mirror wall, vertical pearl light bars, chrome garment rails, and a glowing doorway at frame right.
- **Zone B: Chrome hallway** - the doorway leads into a narrow reflective hallway with diagonal pearl light strips, chrome seams, floor tape, and a visible stage threshold at the far end.
- **Zone C: Performance stage** - the hallway opens into a black-chrome reflective floor, three central pearl light panels, five member-mapped light columns, and chrome arches framing the final pose.

Character progression:

- **Rae** begins as the calm center at the mirror, becomes the alignment point as the group exits, then owns center stage in Panels 7-9.
- **Jia** starts with the chrome jacket adjustment, enters the hallway with sharper attitude, then anchors the aggressive right-side hook angles.
- **Sena** moves from soft backstage reflection to the emotional pre-hook close-up, then frames Rae on stage with a luminous vocalist expression.
- **Nari** starts as the dancer warming up, moves to the threshold as the count leader, then drives the step and pose hits.
- **Yumi** starts as the warm member catching the camera, carries the hallway fan-cam connection, then finishes as the bright audience-facing frame.

Panel-by-panel blocking:

1. Rae is closest to camera at the mirror; the others exist as layered reflections behind her.
2. The camera shifts along the same mirror wall to Jia at the garment rail; Nari is mid-stretch, Sena and Yumi prepare deeper in the room.
3. The room lights turn on and all five pivot toward the glowing doorway, establishing the exit path.
4. Yumi and Sena are first through the hallway, moving camera-left to camera-right; Rae, Jia, and Nari remain readable deeper behind them.
5. Sena pauses at a vanity-like reflection just before the stage threshold, creating the pre-hook breath before the group enters.
6. Nari steps forward and cues the count at the threshold while Rae centers the formation and the other members file into their lanes.
7. The group reaches the stage and forms a diagonal hook line: Rae center, Jia and Nari as sharp anchors, Sena and Yumi framing.
8. The diagonal compresses into a low-angle five-column hit after one forward step, with each member landing under her own pearl light.
9. The formation opens into the final centered group pose beneath the chrome arches, with the stage fully switched on.

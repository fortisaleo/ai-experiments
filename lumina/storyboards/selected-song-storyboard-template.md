# Selected Song Storyboard Template

Use this after choosing one song from `lumina/songs/five-song-slate.md`.

Default recommendation: `Switchlight`.

## Video Format

- Total target: 60 seconds.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16.
- Resolution: 720p.
- Structure: four 15-second Seedance reference-to-video clips.
- Inputs per clip: selected song audio segment, member references from `experiments/003-kpop-idol-beauty-ugc/personalities.json`, optional lookbook/storyboard references.

## Clip 1: Backstage Ignition

Time: 0-15s

Purpose: Establish LUMINA as a proper group, not a single beauty ad.

Prompt draft:

```text
@Audio1 is the selected LUMINA song intro and first verse. Five fictional original K-pop group members prepare backstage in a pearl pink and chrome dressing room. Rae starts calm at the mirror, Yumi smiles near the hallway light, Sena sings softly under vanity bulbs, Jia adjusts a chrome jacket, and Nari stretches before stepping toward stage. Vertical 9:16 music-video framing, handheld but polished, no real celebrity likeness, no readable logos, no captions.
```

## Clip 2: Pre-Hook Lift

Time: 15-30s

Purpose: Move from private backstage nerves into stage momentum.

Prompt draft:

```text
@Audio1 is the selected LUMINA pre-hook. The members leave the backstage room in sequence, walking through a chrome hallway with pearl pink light pulsing to the beat. Sena leads the melodic lift, Rae gives a center glance, Yumi catches the camera warmly, Jia adds sharper attitude, and Nari cues the first dance count. Preserve fictional original identities, no real celebrity likeness, no readable text, no logos.
```

## Clip 3: Main Hook Performance

Time: 30-45s

Purpose: Deliver the most replayable 15-second vertical clip.

Prompt draft:

```text
@Audio1 is the selected LUMINA hook. Full group performance on a chrome stage with pearl pink light panels. The choreography is original and simple: step, pose, hand-light gesture, turn, group hit. Jia and Nari drive the sharp hook movement, Rae holds center polish, Sena supports with vocal close-ups, Yumi gives a bright fan-cam smile. Music-video energy, clean camera cuts, no choreography copied from real groups, no readable logos or captions.
```

## Clip 4: Final Tag And Group Identity

Time: 45-60s

Purpose: End with a memorable title/tag frame and reusable group identity.

Prompt draft:

```text
@Audio1 is the selected LUMINA final chorus and outro. The performance moves from stage to a glossy backstage-stage hybrid set. Each member gets a fast close-up, then the group lands in one final original pose under pearl pink and chrome lights. Optional Lumora glow-serum prop appears only as a small frosted bottle on the vanity edge with no readable label. End on a confident group glance into camera. No real celebrity likeness, no readable text, no logos, no clinical skincare claims.
```

## Acceptance Checklist

- The title hook is visually obvious within Clip 3.
- All five members appear as a group.
- The video reads as a music video first, not just a product ad.
- Lumora appears only if it supports the world.
- No prompt asks for real idol likenesses or exact real choreography.

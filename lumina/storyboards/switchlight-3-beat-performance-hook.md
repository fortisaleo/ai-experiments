1. **Mirror Charge** (`0.0-3.5s`)  
   Rae anchors the mirror. The other four members appear through reflection and dressing-room depth. One shared breath starts the transformation as dim pearl bulbs begin to glow.

2. **Hallway Switch** (`3.5-7.5s`)  
   The group advances through the chrome hallway. Nari cues the original switchlight gesture: hand rises near shoulder, wrist flicks down like a light switch, pearl lights ignite behind them in sequence.

3. **Stage Hook / Final Command** (`7.5-15.0s`)  
   Full group on the chrome stage. Rae holds center, Jia and Nari drive sharp hook energy, Sena and Yumi frame the formation. The group repeats the switchlight gesture, steps forward into the `Step, pose, shine` hit, then holds the final hero pose for the last second.

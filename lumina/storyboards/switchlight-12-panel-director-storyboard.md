# Switchlight 12-Panel Director Storyboard

Status: canonical full-song director board target

## Premise

`Switchlight` is a full-song comeback MV about LUMINA becoming their stage selves. The story starts with private backstage nerves, builds through a shared hallway ignition, and resolves in controlled stage command.

This board is modeled after a director storyboard sheet, not a clean image contact sheet. Each panel should look hand-drawn, with production annotations, motion arrows, shot labels, lighting notes, lyric cues, and emotional emphasis.

## Format

- Storyboard panel format: horizontal 16:9.
- Sheet layout: 12 panels in a 4x3 grid.
- Visual style: pencil-and-marker director board, grayscale sketch base with selective colored arrows and notes.
- Annotation legend:
  - Red arrows: body movement.
  - Blue arrows: camera movement.
  - Green notes: framing and composition.
  - Orange arrows: lighting.
  - Purple notes: lyric, vocal, and emotional emphasis.
- Safety: no real idol names, no real celebrity likenesses, no copied choreography, no readable third-party logos, no captions inside the fictional scene, no Lumora or product props.

## Physical Geography

The story takes place in one connected venue:

1. Dressing room: mirror wall, dim pearl bulbs, chrome garment rails, black marble counter.
2. Threshold: the glowing doorway out of the dressing room.
3. Chrome hallway: reflective black floor, diagonal pearl light strips, floor tape, stage haze.
4. Stage: chrome arches, pearl light panels, five member-mapped light columns, reflective black-chrome floor.

## Character Movement

- Rae: mirror center -> group alignment point -> calm stage center.
- Jia: jacket spark -> sharper hallway edge -> rap/hook side anchor.
- Sena: private softness -> pre-hook self-recognition -> luminous stage frame.
- Nari: warmup -> threshold count leader -> movement driver.
- Yumi: warm hallway connection -> fan-facing bridge -> final bright side frame.

## 12 Panels

1. **Mirror Before The Switch**  
   Lyric: "Back door, heartbeat, mirror light"  
   Wide dressing-room establishing shot. Rae at the mirror, other members as layered reflections. Camera slow push. Dim pearl bulbs, chrome rails. Emotion: contained nerves.

2. **Chrome Sleeve / First Spark**  
   Lyric: "Chrome on my sleeve like a satellite"  
   Medium Jia wardrobe beat. Jia snaps or adjusts chrome jacket; Rae visible in mirror; Nari warming up behind. Handheld drift. Emotion: attitude starts.

3. **One Breath**  
   Lyric: "One breath, then the whole room shines"  
   Group breath beat. All five pause as bulbs ignite in sequence. Camera widens. Body arrows show members turning toward doorway.

4. **Stepping Through The Line**  
   Lyric: "LUMINA stepping through the line"  
   Threshold composition. Group exits dressing room into chrome hallway. Rae leads center line; Yumi glances back; Nari marks first count. Light path pulls them forward.

5. **Hallway Glow**  
   Lyric: "I was quiet in the hallway glow"  
   Yumi/Sena medium two-shot. Deep chrome corridor with diagonal pearl lights. Camera tracks backward. Emotion: private vulnerability inside forward motion.

6. **Speakers Blow / Color Rise**  
   Lyric: "Counting every second till the speakers blow / Sena sings it soft, let the color rise"  
   Sena vocal close-up with Yumi crossing behind. Hair and fabric motion. Pink light bloom begins. Close lens and shallow depth. Emotion: confidence starting to surface.

7. **Vanity Future**  
   Lyric: "See the future in the vanity mirror"  
   Stylized mirror panel near stage threshold. Sena touches mirror/reflection; Rae appears centered behind her. Lighting arrow from mirror to stage. Emotion: self-recognition.

8. **Switch The Light**  
   Lyric: "Tell the whole room, switch the light"  
   Nari count-leader panel. Low-angle threshold shot. Nari hand lifts; group locks into lanes behind her. Pearl panels switch on downstage. Story pivot.

9. **Hook Formation**  
   Lyric: "Switchlight, switchlight / Turn me on when the beat hits right"  
   First full stage reveal. Group diagonal formation. Rae center, Jia/Nari sharp anchors, Sena/Yumi framing. Original light-switch hand gesture. Stabilized push or crane.

10. **Step, Pose, Shine**  
   Lyric: "Step, pose, shine, don't blink"  
   Low-angle performance hit. One forward step lands into a clean pose. Five member-mapped light columns flare. Strong body arrows, floor reflections, precise commercial timing.

11. **Camera Catch / Rap Charge**  
   Lyric: "Click, click, camera, catch my angle / Nari on the count, Jia on the flame"  
   Jia/Nari kinetic rap panel. Whip-pan or orbit. Jia cuts toward lens; Nari crosses behind with count gesture. More aggressive camera grammar, still polished.

12. **Where The Lights Go Bright**  
   Lyric: "Meet us where the lights go bright"  
   Final wide hero shot. Full group under chrome arch, stage fully switched on. Rae calm center, everyone locked into final identity. Single hard spotlight plus pearl field. Emotion: command, not chaos.

# Switchlight

Status: selected first LUMINA single

Target length: 65 seconds

## Positioning

`Switchlight` is LUMINA's first broader pop single: chrome house-pop about crossing from backstage nerves into stage power. It keeps the pearl pink and chrome world from the Lumora experiment, but the song is written to stand alone as a group track rather than an ad hook.

## Member Intent

- Rae opens as the calm center.
- Yumi and Sena carry the private backstage verse.
- Sena lifts the pre-hook into confidence.
- Jia and Nari sharpen the hook and rap tag.
- The full group lands the title phrase as the reusable short-form moment.
